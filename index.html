About

With a diverse background in both psychology and computer programming, I bring a unique perspective to the world of software development. My educational journey began at Michigan State University (MSU), where I delved into the complexities of the human mind, studying psychology. This experience provided me with profound insights into thought processes, memory, and how we shape our world through our thoughts. This understanding of human behavior and cognition plays a pivotal role in my approach to software development, particularly in creating user-centric applications and designing real-world solutions.
